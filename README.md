### 👨‍💻 The Intersection of Physics & Code

With a background in Computer Science and over **six years of professional software engineering experience**, I am currently bridging the gap between production-level coding standards and complex many-body physics. My research focuses on accelerating the discovery of magnetic materials through Geometric Deep Learning and first-principles data.

- 🔭 **Current Research:** Developing **DSpinGNN**, a custom Equivariant Graph Neural Network built from scratch in PyTorch/e3nn, designed to simulate spin-lattice dynamics and magnetization coupling in 2D materials (CrI3).
- ⚙️ **Scientific Tooling:** Independently developed **Phystrackx**, a proprietary video-tracking software designed to streamline data acquisition in experimental physics labs.
- 🔬 **The Goal:** Translating physical symmetries into highly efficient, scalable code to push the boundaries of statistical mechanics and thermal property predictions.
- 🌱 **Beyond the Screen:** I spend my downtime listening to audiobooks, exploring strategic philosophy, and going on long, calm drives.
